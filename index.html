Tips to boost your productivity

Study tip

The 2-minute rule for tasks

If a task takes less than two minutes, do it now. This simple rule prevents small tasks from piling up and overwhelming your list.

Technique

How to use the Pomodoro technique

Work for 25 minutes, rest for 5. After four cycles take a longer break. This rhythm keeps your focus sharp and prevents mental fatigue.

Planning

Plan your week every Sunday

A 15-minute weekly review every Sunday sets you up for a calm, productive week. Map out your subjects and protect your study blocks.
